Suppose that you picked 10 and other participants in your group picked 20, 20, 10, 30. The average of all chosen numbers is 18. Which number did the winner pick and what would be your payoff?

Solution: The winners picked 10 and your payoff would be 50 points.

Explanation: The average of all chosen numbers was (10+20+20+10+30)/5=18 as given. Two-third of 18 was 18/3*2=12. 10 was the closest chosen number to 12. Thus you and the otherparticipant who picked it were the winners. Each of you would receive 50 points.
